Financial Foundation

Build your business on a strong financial foundation.

This one-time service is designed for new businesses or business owners who are ready to get organized. Together, we'll establish a bookkeeping system that supports accurate financial records and prepares your business for long-term success.

Service Includes:

  • Initial bookkeeping consultation
  • QuickBooks Online setup or organization (if needed)
  • Bank and credit card account connections
  • Chart of Accounts setup and organization
  • Review and organization of existing financial records
  • A bookkeeping system designed for ongoing success

Monthly Bookkeeping

Reliable bookkeeping that keeps your business moving forward.

Ongoing monthly bookkeeping keeps your financial records accurate, organized, and current, giving you confidence in your numbers while allowing you to focus on running your business.

Monthly Bookkeeping Includes:

  • Organizing and categorizing income and expenses
  • Bank and credit card reconciliations
  • Maintaining accurate, up-to-date financial records
  • Monthly Profit & Loss Statement
  • Monthly Balance Sheet
  • Ongoing bookkeeping support

QuickBooks Online Services

Get the most out of your bookkeeping software.

Whether you're new to QuickBooks Online or need assistance maintaining your account, I can help ensure your bookkeeping system remains organized and functions the way it should.

Services Include:

  • QuickBooks Online setup
  • Account organization
  • Connected account maintenance
  • Troubleshooting and general support

Not Sure Which Service Is Right for You?

Every business is different, and your bookkeeping needs may change as you grow. If you're unsure where to begin or what level of support is right for your business, I'd be happy to discuss your goals and recommend the best path forward.
