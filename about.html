Services

Welcome! I'm Kristen Lucero, owner of True North Books Solutions.

I started True North Books Solutions to help small and growing businesses have accurate, organized financial records they can rely on—without the complexity or overhead of a large accounting firm. I believe bookkeeping should be consistent, organized, and reliable, giving business owners confidence that their financial information is up to date whenever they need it.

Based in Jurupa Valley, I work with businesses throughout the Inland Empire and remotely across California. I specialize in QuickBooks Online and provide monthly bookkeeping, catch-up services, and financial reporting tailored to each client's needs.

When you work with True North Books Solutions, you'll work directly with me. I take pride in building long-term relationships through clear communication, attention to detail, and dependable service. My goal is to provide bookkeeping you can trust, so you can have confidence in your books while focusing on the many other responsibilities that come with running a business.

I look forward to getting to know you and learning how I can support your business.
